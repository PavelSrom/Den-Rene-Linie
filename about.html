Our story

More than 30 years ago, Mai-Britt Gregersen started the cleaning company Den Rene Linie in Aalborg. The company, which today has 25 employees, is one of Aalborgs oldest cleaning companies, and René Gregersen has been able to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the anniversary of Den Rene Linie, which his mother established in 1986. He took over after Mai-Britt Gregersen’s early death and was only 22 years old at the time.

Rene Gregersen

Founder of Den Rene Linie

We still have customers that my mother got contracted with in 1986, and we are incredibly proud of that. If customers last for 30 years, we must be doing something right.

Our essence

Den Rene Linie is a cleaning company that has a strong focus on quality assurance, flexible solutions and contact with customers.

„We provide flexible cleaning solutions and care that our customers get the right employee for the right job, and that it is the same worker who is up and running from time to time. Our staff have been through thorough training, which ensures professional competency, and we only use approved cleaning agents. For the past 15 years, we have traded cleaning products at Stadsing A/S, which is one of Denmark's largest in the field. We use eco-friendly and Nordic Swan-labeled products as much as possible. It’s important that we protect the environment.“
