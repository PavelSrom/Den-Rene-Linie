Frequently asked questions:

Do you also do private cleaning?

Yes, while it is possible as we are very flexible private cleaning is not always an option for us currently. Contact us to find out if we can clean your home!

How many employees do you have?

We have 24 employees and counting!

How did you start the company?

After my mother’s death in 1987 I took over the business for her. Visit our About Us page to find out more.

How many years have you been doing this?

We have been officially cleaning for more than 27 years.

Do you clean outside of Aalborg?

Yes we currently offer commercial cleaning services in Aalborg, Stovring, Norresundby and Hobro.

When can you start?

Today!

Does anyone need to be present during the cleaning?

After you book a cleaning, you are prompted to give us entry instructions and provide us with a key and/or entry code. Then, whether you would like to be present or not, it is completely up to you.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

We only use and provide environmentally friendly cleaning services and solutions. Almost any equipment is provided depending on your needs. Apart from that, we do not provide: vacuum or any other heavy duty cleaning equipment unless asked.

Any unanswered questions?

We would love any question you might have!
