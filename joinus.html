We're hiring:
CLEANING ASSISTANTS

At Den Rene Linie we are always happy to hire motivated people to join our team and start making clients happy together!

The job

The job covers all kinds of cleaning tasks for our regular and new clients – from floor cleaning and dusting tables to toilet cleaning and more specialized cleaning . We are searching for full time employees, part-time employees and replacements during vacation or illnesses.

Working with us is full of benefits including flexible working hours as well as a supportive and friendly team!

Requirements:

  • Danish and/or English on a communicative level
  • No criminal record
  • High standards of work
  • Driver's license (optional)

How to apply:

Please fill out the form and attach a motivational letter and your CV – either in Danish or English. We don't accept application by phone.
